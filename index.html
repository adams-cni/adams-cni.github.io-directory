Public Officials Directory


Capitol News Illinois exists to bring credible and unbiased coverage of state government to the people of Illinois. As part of this mission, we want to make it easy to understand who oversees state government. This directory will tell you exactly who leads each of the state's three branches of government and provide information about them, their role, their campaign finances and conflicts of interest.
